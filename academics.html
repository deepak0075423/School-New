Evaluation

Assessment & Examination System

Fair, comprehensive, and stress-free evaluation that focuses on understanding, not just marks.

Formative Assessment

Conducted 4 times a year through class tests, assignments, oral exams, and projects. Accounts for 40% of final grade.

Summative Assessment

Half-yearly and annual examinations covering the complete syllabus. Accounts for 60% of the grade. Strict CBSE pattern.

Continuous Tracking

AI-powered performance dashboard tracks every student. Parents get weekly reports. Teachers get actionable insights.
