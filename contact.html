FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries. Can't find yours? Send us a message above.

How do I apply for admission to Vidyalaya ERP?
You can apply online through our Admissions page. Fill in the 4-step enrollment form with the student's personal details, academic records, and upload required documents. Our admissions team will review and contact you within 3 working days.
How do I access the parent or student portal?
Visit our Login page and select the appropriate role (Student or Parent). Use the credentials provided at the time of admission. For credential recovery or portal access issues, contact our IT support at itsupport@vidyalayaerp.edu.in.
What documents are required for new student admission?
Required documents include: birth certificate, previous school transfer certificate (TC), last class marksheets, Aadhar card (student and parent), 4 passport-size photographs, and medical fitness certificate. All documents should be self-attested. Original documents are verified at the time of enrollment.
How can parents schedule a meeting with teachers?
Parents can schedule meetings directly through the Parent Portal — log in, go to the Messages section, and use the "Schedule a Call" feature to book a convenient time slot. Alternatively, you can call the school office to arrange a parent-teacher meeting during PTM days (held every alternate month).
How do I pay school fees online?
Fees can be paid through the Parent Portal under the Fees section. We accept UPI, Net Banking, credit/debit cards, and demand drafts. For any payment discrepancies or receipt queries, contact our Accounts department at accounts@vidyalayaerp.edu.in.
Are there scholarships or fee concessions available?
Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships for students scoring above 90% in board exams, and need-based concessions for economically weaker sections (EWS). The RTE quota provides free education for eligible students. Contact the Accounts office for details and application forms.
