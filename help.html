FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Browse questions by portal or topic. Click any question to expand the answer.

What is Vidyalaya ERP?
Vidyalaya ERP is a comprehensive School Management System that digitises daily school operations — attendance, results, fee management, communication, LMS, HR, payroll, and more. It provides dedicated portals for students, parents, teachers, and admins.
How do I get login credentials?
Login credentials are issued by the school administration:
  • Students & Parents — credentials are shared via registered email/SMS at the time of admission.
  • Teachers & Staff — credentials are provided by the HR department on joining.
If you haven't received credentials, contact the school office or raise a support ticket below.
What browsers are supported?
Vidyalaya ERP works best on Google Chrome (v90+), Mozilla Firefox (v85+), Microsoft Edge, and Safari (v14+). Internet Explorer is not supported. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.
Is there a mobile app?
The web portal is fully responsive and works well on smartphones and tablets. A dedicated Android and iOS app is currently under development and will be available soon. You can bookmark the portal on your home screen for quick access.
How is my data kept secure?
All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. We follow DPDP (Digital Personal Data Protection) Act guidelines. Role-based access control ensures users only see data they are authorised to view.
How do I log in to the Student Portal?
Go to the Login page and select the Student tab. Enter your Student ID (shared at admission) and your password. If you haven't received credentials, contact your class teacher or the admin office.
Where can I see my attendance?
After logging in, click Attendance in the left sidebar. You'll see a monthly calendar view with colour-coded days (green = present, red = absent, yellow = late), along with your overall attendance percentage per subject.
How do I download study material?
Go to My Courses in the sidebar. Click on any subject card to open it. You'll find PDFs, notes, videos, and assignments uploaded by your teacher. Click the download icon next to any file to save it.
How do I check my exam results?
Results are published in the Results section of your dashboard once your teacher or admin makes them available. You can view marks, grades, and rank for each subject. Past results from previous terms are also accessible.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your registered email address. A reset link will be sent to your email. If you don't receive it within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact the school IT support at itsupport@vidyalayaerp.edu.in.
How do I track my child's attendance?
Log in to the Parent Portal, then navigate to the Attendance section. You'll see a weekly and monthly breakdown of your child's attendance, with subject-wise details. You'll also receive SMS/email alerts for each absence.
How do I pay school fees online?
Go to the Fees section in the Parent Portal. You'll see all pending dues, due dates, and installment options. Click Pay Now and choose from UPI, Net Banking, or Credit/Debit card. A payment receipt is generated instantly and sent to your registered email.
How do I message my child's teacher?
In the Parent Portal, go to Messages. Select the teacher from the dropdown list and type your message. For a more formal meeting, use the Schedule a Call feature to book a time slot that suits both parties.
Can I link multiple children to one parent account?
Yes. If you have more than one child enrolled, the Parent Portal will show a child switcher at the top of the dashboard. Contact the admin office to link additional children to your parent account.
How do I download fee receipts?
Go to Fees → Payment History. Each completed payment shows a Download Receipt button. Receipts are in PDF format and include the payment reference number, date, and amount.
How do I upload content to the LMS?
In the Teacher Portal, go to LMS in the sidebar. Click Upload Content, select the subject and class, choose the file type (PDF, Video, Assignment, or Link), and click Upload. Students will be notified automatically.
How do I mark attendance for my class?
Navigate to Attendance in your sidebar. Select the class and date. You'll see a list of all students — click P (Present), A (Absent), or L (Late) for each student. Use the Mark All Present button to speed up the process, then adjust individual records. Click Submit to save.
How do I apply for leave?
Go to Leave in the Teacher Portal. Click Apply for Leave, fill in the leave type (Casual, Medical, etc.), select dates, and provide a reason. Your application will be submitted to the admin for approval. You'll receive a notification once it's approved or rejected.
Where can I view my payslip?
Go to Payroll in the sidebar. You'll see your current month's salary breakdown (Basic, HRA, DA, Deductions, Net Pay) and a history of past payslips. Click Download PDF to save any payslip.
How do I complete my self-appraisal?
Navigate to Appraisal in the sidebar during the appraisal window (typically April–May). Rate yourself on the displayed criteria using the star rating system and add self-comments. Submit before the deadline. Your admin will review and provide final ratings.
How do I approve new enrollment requests?
In the Admin Panel, go to Enrollment Management. New applications appear with status "Pending". Click the application to review documents and details. Use the Approve or Reject button. Approved students will receive login credentials via email automatically.
How do I approve or reject teacher leave?
Go to Leave Approvals in the Admin Panel. Pending leave requests are listed with the teacher name, leave type, dates, and reason. Click Approve or Reject. The teacher will be notified immediately via portal notification and email.
How do I post a school-wide announcement?
Go to Announcements in the Admin Panel. Click New Announcement, write your title and message, select the target audience (All / Students / Parents / Teachers), and click Post. The announcement appears in all selected users' notification feeds immediately.
How do I generate attendance reports?
Navigate to Attendance Reports under the Reports section. Select the class, date range, and report type (daily/monthly/subject-wise). Click Generate Report. Reports can be viewed on-screen or exported as Excel/PDF.
How do I add or edit the holiday calendar?
Go to Holidays in the Admin Panel. Click Add Holiday, enter the date and occasion name, and save. Students, parents, and teachers will see the updated calendar in their respective dashboards. You can also bulk-import holidays via CSV.
The portal is not loading. What should I do?
  • Try a hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser or incognito/private window
  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers)
If the issue persists, raise a ticket below.
I'm locked out after too many failed login attempts.
Accounts are temporarily locked for 15 minutes after 5 consecutive failed login attempts. Wait 15 minutes and try again. If you don't remember your password, use the Forgot Password link on the login page. For immediate assistance, contact IT Support.
File upload is failing in the Documents section.
Ensure your file:
  • Is in an accepted format: PDF, JPG, JPEG, or PNG
  • Does not exceed 5MB in size
  • Has no special characters in the filename
Try renaming the file to something simple (e.g. birth_cert.pdf) and upload again. If it still fails, try a different browser.
I'm not receiving email notifications.
Check your spam/junk folder first. Emails are sent from noreply@vidyalayaerp.edu.in — add this to your contacts. If you're still not receiving emails, ensure the registered email address in your profile is correct. Contact IT support to re-verify your email.
Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Fixes

Step-by-step solutions for the most frequently reported problems.

Can't Log In

  1. Check you're on the correct role tab (Student / Parent / Teacher)
  2. Ensure Caps Lock is off when entering password
  3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset via email
  4. Wait 15 min if account is locked after failed attempts
  5. Contact admin if credentials were never received

Page Not Loading

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Hard refresh with Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R
  3. Clear browser cache (Settings → Clear Data)
  4. Try Chrome or Firefox if using another browser
  5. Disable browser extensions temporarily

File Upload Failing

  1. Confirm file is PDF, JPG, or PNG format
  2. Ensure file size is under 5MB
  3. Rename file — remove spaces & special characters
  4. Try uploading from a desktop instead of mobile
  5. Raise a ticket if the issue persists

No Email / SMS Alerts

  1. Check spam/junk folder in your email
  2. Add noreply@vidyalayaerp.edu.in to contacts
  3. Verify registered email/phone in your profile
  4. Check notification settings in the portal
  5. Contact IT support to re-send verification

Mobile Display Issues

  1. Use the latest Chrome or Safari on mobile
  2. Enable JavaScript in browser settings
  3. Rotate to landscape for wide tables
  4. Zoom out if content appears cut off
  5. Bookmark the site on your home screen for app-like access

Payment Failed

  1. Check if amount was debited from your bank first
  2. Wait 10 min — payment may still be processing
  3. Verify internet was stable during transaction
  4. Try a different payment method (UPI / Net Banking)
  5. Contact Accounts dept with transaction reference
